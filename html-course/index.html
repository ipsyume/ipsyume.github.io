About Me

I'm a B.Tech graduate in Computer Science & Engineering from Shoolini University (Yogananda School of AI, Computer and Data Science), and an incoming Master's student at Professor Tony Lee's Computer Graphics Laboratory at NCKU, Taiwan. My capstone project PhysiGS built a physically-based inverse rendering system on top of 3D Gaussian Splatting, achieving over 307 FPS rendering at 25.33 dB PSNR on a single NVIDIA T4 GPU.
